# 🚀 AI Resume Intelligence SaaS

> The next-generation AI-powered Resume Analyzer that helps candidates understand ATS score, improve resumes, and predict job fit instantly.

---

## 🌐 Live Product Vision

AI Resume Intelligence SaaS simulates a real-world Applicant Tracking System (ATS) used by top companies to screen candidates — powered by Machine Learning + Semantic AI.

---

## ✨ Why This Product?

Most candidates:
- ❌ Don’t know why they get rejected
- ❌ Don’t understand ATS systems
- ❌ Miss key skills recruiters expect

This tool solves this problem using AI.

---

## ⚡ Key Features

### 📄 Smart Resume Analysis
Upload your resume and get instant AI-powered insights.

### 🧠 ATS Scoring Engine
Real-time match score between resume and job description.

### 🎯 Job Role Prediction
AI predicts best-fit job role based on skills.

### 💼 Hire Probability Score
Estimates chances of getting shortlisted.

### 🔍 Skill Gap Detection
Find missing skills required for target job.

### 📊 Visual Analytics Dashboard
Interactive charts and metrics for better understanding.

### 📑 Resume Comparison Mode
Compare two resumes and identify the stronger one.
